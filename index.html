About Shehawy Wellness Group LLC

Shehawy Wellness Group LLC is a Florida-based mobile wellness and preventive healthcare company dedicated to bringing modern, personalized services directly to you.

Our mission is to deliver convenient, high-quality care through a holistic approach that integrates wellness, dental hygiene, IV hydration, and personalized health support. Whether you're focused on long-term prevention, better daily performance, or recovery after intensive activity, our services are designed around your lifestyle.

We coordinate with trusted medical and dental professionals and follow safe, evidence-based protocols so that your wellness experience feels both premium and clinically grounded.

Our Services

We offer flexible, mobile-friendly services designed to fit busy lives while keeping health and wellness at the center.

  • Mobile Dental Hygiene

    Professional cleaning, stain removal, and preventive hygiene services coordinated with licensed providers.

  • IV Hydration & Vitamin Therapy

    Wellness-focused IV drips and vitamin support aimed at recovery, energy, and overall well-being.

  • Home & On-Site Wellness Visits

    Convenient in-home or on-location wellness visits for individuals, families, and small groups.

  • Smile Design Coordination

    Digital smile design planning and coordination with trusted dental labs and clinicians.

  • Athlete & Team Support

    Recovery-focused support tailored for athletes and active individuals.
